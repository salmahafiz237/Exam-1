Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and investing in real estate

You're ready to buy when you have stable income, a good credit score (620+), a down payment saved (10–20%), and plan to stay in the area for 3–5 years. Our team can help assess your financial readiness and connect you with pre-approval services.

Spring and early summer (March–June) are traditionally the best times to sell. However, market conditions and your personal timeline matter more than season. We'll help you determine optimal timing based on your local market.

Your offer should be based on comparable sales, the home's condition, and current market conditions. Our agents provide a detailed comparative market analysis (CMA) to help you make a competitive yet reasonable offer.

Closing costs typically range from 2–5% of the purchase price and include loan origination, appraisal, title insurance, and taxes. Both buyers and sellers have closing costs, though they vary by location and can be negotiated.

Absolutely. A professional home inspection ($300–$600) can save you thousands by identifying potential issues before you buy. We recommend inspections for all property types and can connect you with licensed inspectors.

For buyers, the process typically takes 30–45 days from offer acceptance to closing. For sellers, our properties typically sell within 28 days. We'll provide realistic timelines based on your situation and local market conditions.
